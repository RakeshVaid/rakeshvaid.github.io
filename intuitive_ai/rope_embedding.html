How this works

For even dimension d, RoPE rotates each 2D pair by angle θk(p) = p · base−2k/d where k = 0..(d/2 − 1) and p is the token position. The left plot shows the rotation of each pair on the unit circle at a chosen position; the right plot shows how each pair’s angle evolves from 0 to the current position. Checking “wrap” displays angles mapped into [−π, π].
